ABOUT OUR WEBSITE-

we believe that every one of us can use their skills to make a meaningful contribution. We encourage all those interested in committing time and energy to our cause to check openings or get in touch. If you have the enthusiasm, we would be happy to help find the right fit.

we are focussing to Share the burden on our healthcare system to mitigate the devastating impact of the second wave of Covid-19.

We are augmenting critical supplies to Government Covid Hospitals, stepping up the vaccination drive and extending support services through a National Helpline,” Covid Frontline”. Additionally, we continue our efforts to promote awareness on Covid appropriate behaviour. For almost 1 year, our mission has been to organize the world’s information and make it universally accessible and useful.

Helping people get the right information to stay healthy is more important than ever in the face of a global pandemic like COVID-19.
But now we’ve accelerated our work to help people stay safe, informed and connected.

Few of our objectives are-

  • Helping people find useful information.
  • Protecting people from misinformation.
  • Supporting relief efforts and government organizations.
  • Taking care of our global community.

Have You Lost a Loved One Due to COVID-19?

We are sorry for your loss. Our Virtual Family offers national , state and local information and resources that we hope will help you during this challenging time.

Have You Recovered from COVID-19?

People who have fully recovered from COVID-19 have antibodies in their plasma that may help attack the virus. we are helping to identify prospective donors and manage the distribution of donated convalescent plasma to hospitals treating patients in need.
